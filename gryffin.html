Harry Potter

Gryffindor

History

Godric Gryffindor was the founder of Gryffindor House. His chocolate frog card describes him as the most accomplished dueller of his time and an enlightened fighter against Muggle discrimination.

Traits

The Gryffindor house emphasises the traits of courage as well as \\"daring, nerve, and chivalry,\\" and thus its members are generally regarded as brave, though sometimes to the point of recklessness.

Notable Alumini

The godric gryffindor has been the home to numerous great wizards like Albus Dumbledore, Harry Potter, Hermionie Granger and Ronald Weasley
