Harry Potter

Ravenclaw

History

Rowena Ravenclaw founded Ravenclaw House. She was known as a beautiful and intelligent witch, and her chocolate frog card calls her \\"the most brilliant witch of her time.\\" Rowena valued learning, so she chose students who demonstrated cleverness and wisdom.

Traits

Ravenclaw House prizes learning, wisdom, wit, and intellect in its members. Thus, many Ravenclaws tend to be academically motivated and talented students. Ravenclaws also pride themselves in being original in their ideas, and methods.

Notable Alumini

Helena Ravenclaw Founder,Uric the Oddball and Ignatia Wildsmith are the notable alumini .
