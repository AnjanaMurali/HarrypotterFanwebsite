Hufflepuff

History

Helga Hufflepuff was the founder of Hufflepuff House. Her chocolate frog card describes her as a loyal, charming witch with great skill at performing food-related charms.

Traits

hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play rather than a particular aptitude
