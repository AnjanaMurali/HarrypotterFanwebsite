Harry Potter

Slytherin

History

Slytherin is one of the four Houses at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, founded by Salazar Slytherin.

Traits

Cunning, resourcefulness, leadership, and ambition. Many Slytherin students tend to clique together, often acquiring leaders, which further exemplifies Slytherin's ambitious qualities

Notable Alumini

Lord Voldemort, Horace Slughorn, Bellatrix Lestrange , Severus Snape are the notable alumini .
